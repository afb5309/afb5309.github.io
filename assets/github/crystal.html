Alex's Crystal Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Points score.

You win the game by exactly matching your Points score to the random number, you lose the game if your Points score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
